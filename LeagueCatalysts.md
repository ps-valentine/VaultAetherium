## Chosen Relics
#### Tier 1 
- x4 XP multiplier
- all farming crops grow 5x faster
- run energy never drains
- hit chance is increased by 20%
- archaeology shop is full available
- souls earned from necromancy rituals are increased by 400%
#### Tier 1 Catalyst: Excavator (Mining/Archaeology)
- grants a pickaxe that provides mining and archaeology benefits
- bank ores and archaeology materials automatically when mining and excavating
- ores are automatically smelted into bars, without the required smithing level
- gems are automatically cut when mined
- increased rewards from rockertunities
- archaeology materials can be found within geodes
- archaeology journal acts as a free soil screener
- archaeology time sprites do not move and follow the player
- restoring an artefact costs half as many materials
- receive 5x as many chronotes
#### Tier 2
- x6 XP multiplier
- Thaler quantity earned is 4x
- rare items are twice as common
- when assigned a slayer task, you'll be able to choose between two tasks
- choose between minimum or maximum assignments when requesting a new slayer task
- slayer points and reaper points are 5x when completing tasks
#### Tier 2 Catalyst: Farming Frenzy (Farming)
- a range of benefits to nurture your farming skills
- patches are harvested instantly and produce is automatically noted
- seeds planted instantly grow to full
- 15% extra chance of increased crop yield
- all patches are always treated with ultracompost
- 20% chance to save planted seeds when growing
- herb seeds are planted at max amount with only the base amount of seeds used
#### Tier 3
- x6 XP multiplier
- all movement abilities CD reduced to 4.8s
- dive ability automatically unlocked
#### Tier 3 Catalyst: Assassin's Insight (Teleport/Utility)
- provides a teleport to all major slayer targets
- grants the Skull of Slaying that allows you to teleport to any slayer master, slayer dungeon, and a bunch of slayer monsters.

### Catalysts of Unknown Tiers
#### Ascension (Combat)
- a broad range of reading that can provide some exceptional abilities
- attacks have a 5% chance to empower you with the Stone of Jas, increasing your combat stats to 255 for 6 seconds
- grants the Elder God book, containing the effects of every combat god book, including Erethdor's Grimoire
- god book activation CD reduced from 15 seconds to 4.8 seconds
#### Banker's Note (Utility)
- receive the Banker's Note inventory item
- grants access to your bank almost anywhere in the world
- the 'activate' option allows you to note/unnote items in one action, with a specifiable quantity
- manually use items on the banker's note to note/unnote items directly items directly into inventory
#### Barbarism (Melee)
- melee basic attacks generate 2x adrenaline
- melee ability & special attack CDs are reduced by 20%
- melee attacks have a 100% hit chance
- melee weapon attack range is increased by 1
- melee base damage is increased by 500
- melee damage heals for 10% of the damage dealt
#### Bombardment (Ranged)
- ranged basic attacks generate 2x adrenaline
- ranger ability & special attack CDs are reduced by 20%
- ranged attacks have a 100% hit chance
- single-target ranged attacks will also be cast on up to 9 additional enemies within 1 tile of the target
- 50% chance to save ammunition in combat

#### Crit Happens (Magic)
- magic basic attacks generate 2x adrenaline
- magic ability & special attack CDs are reduced by 20%
- magic attacks have 100% hit chance
- magical critical strike damage is increased by 50%
- 95% chance to save runes in combat
#### Everlasting Faith (Combat/Prayer/Utility)
- gain the ultimate blessing of the gods
- prayer points no longer drain
- protection prayers and deflect curses block +50% damage
- protection prayers and deflect curses act as Soul Split being active
- on death: revive with 100% of your maximum life points; does not work in PvP, 5 minute CD

#### Perkfection (Invention/Utility)
- gain an incredible stack of invention-related boosts
- access to infinite invention materials
- augmented items and machines consume no charge
- helpful perks will trigger 10% more often (doesn't stack with item level 20 benefit)
- invention machines will run twice as fast
- when disassembling or alchemising items manually, gain the resources from both actions (not for machines)
- when disassembling or alchemising items manually, have a 66% chance to save the resource (not for machines)
- unlock all blueprints in the dwarven and goblin tech tree

#### Soulborne (Necromancy)
- necromancy basic attacks generate 2x adrenaline
- necromancy ability CDs are reduced by 20%
- necromancy attacks have 100% hit chance
- necromancy spirits are 25% larger and deal 25% more damage
- 95% chance to save ectoplasm and runes when casting abilities and incantations
- unlock all abilities from the necromancy well of souls talent tree
#### Specialist (Combat)
- grants the Elder Essence of Finality Amulet, which allows you to select any special attack from a menu without needing the weapon
- at a bank, the special attack contained within can be changed for free
- every 15 seconds, your next special attack will consume no adrenaline
- special attacks deal 50% more damage
